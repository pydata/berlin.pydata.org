PyData

PyData is an educational program of NumFOCUS, a 501(c)3 non-profit organization in the United States. PyData provides a forum for the international community of users and developers of data analysis tools to share ideas and learn from each other.

The global PyData network promotes discussion of best practices, new approaches, and emerging technologies for data management, processing, analytics, and visualization. PyData communities approach data science using many languages, including (but not limited to) Python, Julia, and R.

The PyData Code of Conduct governs this meetup. To discuss any issues or concerns relating to the code of conduct or the behavior of anyone at a PyData meetup, please contact NumFOCUS Executive Director Leah Silen (+1 512-222-5449; [email protected]focus.org ) or the local organisers are [email protected]

PyData Berlin

Berlin is renowned for its vibrant tech and startup scene; already a stronghold in Web and mobile application development, Berlin also has a growing community of data engineers and data scientists, among them many Pythonistas. This website gives an overview of PyData related activities in Berlin. Please join our efforts and support building a strong PyData community in Berlin.

PyData Berlin meetup

PyData Berlin has been an active member of the Berlin Data scene since 2014. We organise a monthly meetup on the third Wednesday of each month, usually at different locations, as well as more infrequent events like weekend hackathons and workshops. Please join us at the next PyData Berlin meetup.

If you'd like to speak at the PyData Berlin event, please submit a talk proposal using this form.

PyData Berlin Conference

The PyData Berlin conference is an annual, must-attend, event for all data lovers and python enthusiasts in Berlin. Whether you're a novice or an expert in the field, focus more on engineering, analysis or visualisation the PyData Berlin conference is sure to have content worth seeing for you. In addition to all the great talks and workshops there is no place like PyData Berlin to meetup other like minded people and get to talk to the developers of the libraries we all use and love.
